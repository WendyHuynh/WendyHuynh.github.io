MOMENI RUGS REDESIGN

PROJECT BRIEF

An exploratory project of a a full scale redesign for a family owned company that provides a B2B solution for small and large rug dealers on a global scale.

MY ROLE

I was the sole UI/UX designer on this exploration.

RESEARCH

Customer Pain Points

1. Disconnect between social media and website
2. No styled photos on website
3. Busy
4. Doesn’t feel modern
5. Don’t know purpose of site from first glance

Solutions

1. Grouping the categorizations to simplify the massive load of content
2. Showing more relevant content with CTA

RESULTS

Clearer categorization, more minimal and simplistic.
Cohesive, but would like to standardize button styling.
