NATIONWIDE CONNECT

PROJECT BRIEF

The Nationwide Connect is a single platform application that aims to help shoppers quote and buy insurance with confidence and trust.

MY ROLE

I was the lead visual designer on this application.

Wireframe    UI Design    Prototype

RESEARCH

Past Applications

1. Architecture is not structured to be helpful to the users
2. Tension around users comparing current policy coverage with digital quote
3. Cognitive overload with all many questions on one screen

Customer Pain Points

1. Feel anxious and overwhelmed, not confident
2. Unprepared, unorganized and confused
3. Don’t feel in control of the process

Solutions

1. Encouragement to explore, learn, and gain confidence
2. Reduced sense of the risk of failure 
3. Explanations of why coverage is suitable for the situation
4. Provide information about the value of discounts and allowing customer to opt in
5. A simplified, streamlined process

STYLE GUIDE

The aesthetic was to engender feelings of trustworthiness through vibrant colors and softer accents and fills. White space, composition, and motion design will be used to convey contemporary aesthetic. Illustrations can be used in specific contexts to create helpful moments and contribute to optimism and confidence.

Design Library

My greatest contribution was leading the creation of a component library for clear structure of navigation and tasks. Consistency of information presentation was crucial for such a large design team.

Design Considerations

1. On brand- Expressing the tone, character, and uniqueness of the Nationwide brand visual language. It should feel evolutionary rather than revolutionary
2. On trend- Design language should align with industry and cross-industry interface design trends. Consider what elements of the design can key off current and near-term trends to afford the experiences with a “contemporary” on-trend design language 
3. Modernized components- visualizing interface interaction components that have design elements and functionality that are aligned with current trends in UI design for interaction.
